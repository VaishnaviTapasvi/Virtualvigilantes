Paan Shots

1st September 2023



Firstly, in a mixer jar take 3 betel leaves. Trim off the head and tail of leaf.
Add ¼ cup sugar, 3 pods cardamom, 2 tbsp fennel, 2 tbsp coconut, and 3 drops green food color.
Grind to smooth paste adding 4 tbsp water.
Paan concentrate is ready. You can store it in the refrigerator for a week.
To prepare the paan shots, in a blender take chilled milk.
Add ice cubes and 2 tbsp of prepared paan concentrate.
Blend to a frothy milkshake.
Finally, enjoy paan shots garnished with rooh afza.

Cold Coffee with Ice Cream

1st October 2023



Firstly, in a small cup take 1 tbsp coffee powder, 2 tbsp sugar, and 3 tbsp warm water.
Mix well making sure everything is well combined.
Now in a blender jar take 1½ cup chilled milk, prepared coffee decoction, and 1 scoop ice cream.
Add ice cubes and blend smoothly.
Finally, pour the cold coffee with ice cream if required.

Dry Fruit Lassi

1st November 2023



Firstly, in a blender take 1 cup curd. Make sure the curd is thick and chilled.
Add 2 tbsp pistachios, 2 tbsp almonds, 1 anjeer, 2 tbsp sugar and ¼ tsp saffron food colour.
Blend to make sure everything is well combined.
Finally, pour dry fruit lassi in a tall glass and top with chopped pistachios.

Classic Iced Tea

1st December 2023



Firstly, in a blender take 2 tbsp mint, 3 tsp lemon juice and ½ cup water.
Blend to smooth mint juice. keep aside.
In a tall glass take few ice cubes, 2 slices of lemon and few mint.
Pour in prepared mint juice and chilled tea decoction.
Finally, mix well and enjoy classic iced tea.
